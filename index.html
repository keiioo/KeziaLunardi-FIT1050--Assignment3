Meet Julie Bourke

Born and raised in the heart of Melbourne, Juile Bourke has dominated the musical industry with a unique passion and flair that touches the heart. The depth of her talents are not limited to vocals, but extends to the theatre and acting. She is notably famous for directing, writing and performing complex cabarets that leaves the audience absolutely speechless. Her expert knowledge in this field knows no bounds due to her extensive experience and intensive musical studies in London. Her musical journey continues to evolve, till this day, as she strives to help rising stars develop their talent.

Music

Julie Bourke has composed various different tracks ranging from musical records, pop hits, cinematic backings, orchestral compositions and much more. Art of theatre in Melbourne

Melbourne has a very strong theatre presence, with several different live performances yearly ranging from iconic classics to newer plays. As such, there is a huge potential for rising actors and aspiring theatre students to pursue their acting dreams within Melbourne. As a main player in the scene, Bourke wants to be a driving force for the new generation in theatre aiming to further the art into newer lights for Melbourne.
